### Page 2: Lesson Plan Template

**Subject:** Art & Creativity â€” Blind Box Paper Crafts

**Grade Level:** K-5

**Time:** 45 minutes

**Materials:**
- Blind Box Creator templates (1 per student, printed on cardstock)
- Scissors (student-safe)
- Glue sticks
- Markers/crayons (optional, for customization)

**Learning Objectives:**
- Students will practice fine motor skills (cutting, folding)
- Students will follow multi-step directions
- Students will demonstrate creativity and personal expression
- Students will collaborate and share with peers

**Common Core Alignment:**
- **CCSS.ELA-LITERACY.SL.K-5.1:** Participate in collaborative conversations
- **Visual Arts Standard 1:** Creating (apply skills/techniques to create artwork)
- **Visual Arts Standard 2:** Responding (interpret intent and meaning in artistic work)

**Lesson Flow:**

**Introduction (5 min):**
- Show example blind box: "Who knows what a blind box is?"
- Explain rarity system: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary
- "Today we're making our OWN mystery boxes!"

**Demonstration (10 min):**
- Project template on screen or hold up large printout
- Demonstrate: cutting on solid lines, folding on dotted lines
- Show assembled box: "Ta-da! Now it's your turn!"

**Independent Work (20 min):**
- Distribute templates (1 per student)
- Students cut, fold, and glue their boxes
- Circulate: help struggling students, praise creative choices
- Optional: students customize with markers before assembling

**Sharing (10 min):**
- Students display finished boxes on desks
- Gallery walk: students admire each other's work
- "Who got a Legendary? Who got Common? Let's see!"
- Optional: students trade characters (teach negotiation skills)

**Cleanup (5 min):**
- Collect scraps, return supplies
- Students take boxes home OR display on classroom shelf

**Extension Activities:**
- Write a story about your character (creative writing)
- Create a diorama scene with multiple characters (art)
- Calculate probability of getting each rarity (math)
- Research Japanese kawaii culture (social studies)
