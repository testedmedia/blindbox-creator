## EMAIL DESIGN BEST PRACTICES

### **Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile preview)
- Use emojis (🌸💕🎁) to stand out
- Create curiosity, not clickbait
- A/B test 2 variants every send

### **Preview Text:**
- First 40-50 characters after subject
- Don't duplicate subject line
- Tease value inside email

### **Body:**
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Bold important points
- Bullet lists for scannability
- 1 primary CTA per email
- Mobile-first design (60%+ open on mobile)

### **CTAs:**
- Big buttons (not text links)
- Action-oriented copy ("Download Free Pack" not "Click Here")
- Contrasting colors (pink button on white background)
- Repeat CTA 2-3 times in long emails

## SEGMENTATION STRATEGY

**Segment List By:**

1. **Purchase History**
   - Never purchased (nurture)
   - 1 purchase (upsell)
   - 2+ purchases (VIP)
   - Pro subscriber (retention)

2. **Engagement Level**
   - Highly engaged (open 80%+ emails)
   - Moderately engaged (open 40-80%)
   - Low engaged (open <40%)
   - Inactive (no opens in 60 days)

3. **Interest**
   - Birthday party planners
   - Teachers
   - Homeschool parents
   - Craft enthusiasts

4. **Lifecycle Stage**
   - New subscriber (0-7 days)
   - Active user (8-90 days)
   - At-risk (91-180 days)
   - Churned (180+ days)

**Send targeted emails to each segment for 2x conversion rates!**
