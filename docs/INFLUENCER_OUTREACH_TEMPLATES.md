## Response Rate Benchmarks

| Tier | Target Open Rate | Target Reply Rate | Target Conversion |
|------|------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Nano (1K-10K) | 40%+ | 20%+ | 10%+ |
| Micro (10K-100K) | 30%+ | 10%+ | 5%+ |
| Macro (100K-1M) | 20%+ | 5%+ | 2%+ |
| Mega (1M+) | 10%+ | 2%+ | 1%+ |

**If your rates are lower:**
- [ ] Improve subject line (make it more personal)
- [ ] Shorten email body (under 150 words)
- [ ] Add social proof (testimonial from similar influencer)
- [ ] Send at optimal time (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm their timezone)
- [ ] Follow up (2nd email gets 2x the replies)

## Lemlist/Instantly.ai Setup

### Campaign Settings
- **Daily send limit:** 20-30 emails/day (avoid spam filters)
- **Sending schedule:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm (recipient timezone)
- **Follow-up delay:** Email 2 at +4 days, Email 3 at +8 days
- **Stop on reply:** Yes (don't spam people who replied)
- **Personalization:** Use {{firstName}}, {{recentPost}}, {{platform}} variables

### Email Tracking
- **Open tracking:** ON
- **Link tracking:** ON
- **Reply tracking:** ON
- **Bounce handling:** Auto-remove bounced emails

### A/B Testing
Test 2 subject lines for first 20 sends, then use winner for remaining sends.
