# Growth Strategy — Blind Box Creator
## 15 High-Impact Growth Tasks

**Context:** Kawaii blind box paper craft creator with 75 template packs, AI character generator, 50% affiliate program, EN/ES support. Target: families, teachers, crafters, party planners. Current: v4.0, full catalog live, ready to scale.

**Goal:** 10x traffic and revenue in 90 days through influencer marketing, viral growth loops, and strategic partnerships.

## INFLUENCER OUTREACH (Tasks 1-6)

### Task 1: Build Influencer CRM & Target List (Week 1)
**Why:** Top 1% companies use data-driven influencer selection, not random outreach.

**Action:**
1. Create Notion database with columns: Name, Platform, Followers, Engagement Rate, Niche, Email, Status, Notes
2. Target 4 tiers:
   - **Nano (1K-10K):** 50 influencers — parenting bloggers, craft YouTubers, teacher accounts
   - **Micro (10K-100K):** 20 influencers — family vloggers, DIY creators, mommy bloggers
   - **Macro (100K-1M):** 10 influencers — top craft channels, parenting influencers
   - **Mega (1M+):** 5 dream targets — major family/craft influencers
3. Use tools:
   - HypeAuditor (influencer search + fraud detection)
   - Modash (database of 250M+ influencers with filters)
   - Manual search: TikTok #craftymom #momlife #teachersofinstagram, Instagram #kidscraft #papercraft #kawaii, YouTube "DIY crafts for kids"

**Deliverable:** Notion CRM with 85 qualified influencers, sorted by priority score (engagement × fit × reach).

**Metrics:** Avg engagement rate >3%, audience overlap with target demo (parents 25-45, teachers).

### Task 4: Outreach Automation + Sequences (Week 2)
**Why:** Manual outreach doesn't scale. Automate first touch, personalize follow-ups.

**Action:**
1. Set up Lemlist or Instantly.ai for cold email campaigns:
   - **Subject line A/B test:** "Quick collab idea for [Name]?" vs "Loved your [recent post] — partnership?"
   - **Email 1 (Day 0):** Compliment recent content → introduce product → offer tier package → CTA ("15-min call?")
   - **Email 2 (Day 4):** Soft follow-up → share press kit link → testimonial from another influencer
   - **Email 3 (Day 8):** Final value-add → "If not interested, can you recommend someone?" (referral ask)
2. DM automation for Instagram/TikTok (ManyChat or Phantombuster):
   - Auto-DM after they engage with your content
   - Personalized opener: "Hey [Name]! Saw your [post about X]. We'd love to send you a free Pro account — interested?"
3. Track responses in Notion CRM, move to "Replied" pipeline

**Deliverable:** 3-email sequence live, 85 influencers receiving personalized emails over 8 days.

**Metrics:** 20% open rate, 5% reply rate, 2% conversion to call/partnership.

## CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION (Tasks 13-15)

### Task 13: AI Generator Viral Loop (Week 2)
**Why:** Free tool = top-of-funnel magnet. 90% of AI generator users don't convert — fix that.

**Action:**
1. **Shareable results:**
   - Add "Share My Creation" button → auto-generates Twitter/Instagram post with image + "I made this with @blindboxcreator"
   - Watermark generated images: small "blindbox-creator.com" in corner
   - Create unique URLs for each generation: `/create/abc123` (shareable links)
2. **Convert generators to buyers:**
   - After 3rd generation, show popup: "Love your characters? Turn them into a full pack!" → link to custom pack builder (new feature idea)
   - Email users 24hr after generation: "Still thinking about your [rarity] character? Here's 20% off to print it"
   - Show "Upgrade to Pro" banner: "Unlimited generations + exclusive packs for $9.99/mo"
3. **Gamification:**
   - "Rarity Collection" — track which rarities user has generated, encourage hunting for Legendary
   - Achievements: "5 generations = 1 free pack download"

**Deliverable:** Share buttons on result page, conversion popup, achievement system, watermarked images.

**Metrics:** 30% of generators share (3K shares/mo = 150K impressions), 5% convert to purchase.

### Task 14: Exit-Intent Popups & Cart Abandonment (Week 3)
**Why:** 70% of carts are abandoned. Recover even 10% = massive revenue lift.

**Action:**
1. **Exit-intent popup** (when mouse moves to close tab):
   - "Wait! Don't miss out on [pack name]"
   - Offer: 15% off if they complete checkout in next 10 minutes (countdown timer)
   - Use OptinMonster or Privy
2. **Cart abandonment email sequence:**
   - **Email 1 (1 hour):** "You left something behind!" → show cart items, one-click checkout link
   - **Email 2 (24 hours):** "Still interested? Here's 10% off" → discount code
   - **Email 3 (72 hours):** "Last chance! Your cart expires in 24 hours" → urgency + social proof ("2,500+ families love this pack")
3. **SMS recovery** (if phone number collected):
   - "Hey! You left [pack name] in your cart. Complete checkout here: [link]"

**Deliverable:** Exit-intent popup live, 3-email abandonment sequence in Klaviyo/Mailchimp, SMS setup.

**Metrics:** Recover 10% of abandoned carts (industry avg: 5-10%), $500+ monthly recovered revenue.
