## RICH PINS SETUP

**Enable Product Rich Pins:**
1. Add meta tags to product pages:
```html
<meta property="og:title" content="Chibi Kitty Club — 12 Kawaii Blind Box Templates">
<meta property="og:description" content="Print kawaii kitten blind boxes at home! 12 characters, rarity system, assembly guide. $4.99">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://blindbox-creator.vercel.app/products/packs/chibi-kitty-club.png">
<meta property="og:price:amount" content="4.99">
<meta property="og:price:currency" content="USD">
```

2. Validate at pinterest.com/business/validate
3. Apply for Rich Pins
4. Pins will auto-show price + availability

**Result:** 30% higher CTR with Rich Pins vs. standard pins

## ANALYTICS & TRACKING

### **Pinterest Analytics Dashboard**
Track weekly:
- **Impressions:** How many times pins were seen
- **Saves:** How many people saved pins (high intent!)
- **Clicks:** How many clicked through to site
- **CTR:** Clicks / Impressions (target: 0.5%+)
- **Save Rate:** Saves / Impressions (target: 2%+)

### **Google Analytics**
- Set up UTM parameters: `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=[board_name]`
- Track Pinterest traffic in GA4
- Monitor: Bounce rate, time on page, conversions

### **Top Pins Report**
- Monthly: Identify top 10 performing pins
- **Double down:** Create 5 more pins like top performers
- **Kill losers:** Stop promoting pins with <0.2% CTR
