## FILMING TIPS

### Equipment
- **Phone camera** (no fancy gear needed)
- **Natural lighting** (film near window, 10am-2pm)
- **Tripod** ($15 Amazon tripod or stack of books)

### Shooting
- **Vertical format** (9:16, TikTok native)
- **Text overlays** (use TikTok's built-in text tool)
- **Fast cuts** (3-5sec per clip max, keep it snappy)
- **Trending sounds** (check TikTok Discover page daily)

### Editing
- **CapCut** (free, easy, built-in effects)
- **Add captions** (80% watch without sound)
- **Transitions** (snap, swipe, zoom)
- **Effects** (sparkle overlay for "magic" moments)
